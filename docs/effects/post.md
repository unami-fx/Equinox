### Chromatic Abberation

Chromatic Abberation is a type of color distortion caused by the offset of RGB channels.

![preview](../assets/effects/ca.jpg)<br>
<b>Parameters</b><br>

<b>Abberation method</b> - Type of CA<br>
<b>Red offset</b> - Offset of channel R<br>
<b>Green offset</b> - Offset of channel G<br>
<b>Blue offset</b> - Offset of channel B<br>
<b>Distortion factor</b> - Multiplier offset of all channels<br>
